Famous Resturants in Andhra pradesh

The Park Visakhapatnam

Location: Beach Road, Visakhapatnam.
Reviews: The Park Visakhapatnam is known for its scenic location by the beach. Guests often praise the beautiful views, excellent service, and luxurious amenities.

Novotel Visakhapatnam Varun Beach

Location: Beach Road, Visakhapatnam.
Reviews: This upscale hotel offers stunning ocean views and is known for its comfortable rooms and great dining options. The service is highly rated by guests.

The Gateway Hotel MG Road, Vijayawada

Location: MG Road, Vijayawada.
Reviews: This hotel is known for its central location and good service, making it a suitable choice for visitors to Vijayawada.
